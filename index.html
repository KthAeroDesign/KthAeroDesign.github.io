About us

We are KTH Aero Design — a student‑run Design, Build, Fly organization established in 2024. Our mission is to apply classroom theory to real aerospace engineering challenges through hands‑on projects.

Our sponsors

SARC — Swedish Aerospace Research Center

SARC is a strategic collaboration of Swedish universities and research organisations conducting research and education within the aerospace sector. LiU, Chalmers, KTH, LTU and LU are collaborating to shape the future of Swedish aerospace.

Vaxholm Komposit

One of the foremost companies in composite manufacturing in Sweden. They supply the team with a composite workshop as well as crucial know‑how.

Försvarets Materielverk (FMV)

FMV procures, develops, and delivers equipment and services to the Swedish Armed Forces. Specialists in technology, business, law, and project management work together to meet defence needs in peace, crisis, and war.
